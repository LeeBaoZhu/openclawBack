## 批评与争议

### Hacker News 批评（2021）

**批评1：没有原创想法**
> "I've never read anything that wasn't a bad regurgitation of ideas better presented elsewhere from this guy, and I have to wonder why he has amassed such a following."

**回应视角**：
> "If he offers me a bunch of true and useful ideas, in a single place, and in a useful form, that seems valuable to me."

**分析**：批评者认为Naval只是"合成者"而非原创者；支持者认为"聚合+清晰表达"本身就是价值。

---

**批评2：傲慢/说教**
> "Naval always comes off as pretentious/didactic, in my opinion. I respect his accomplishments and ideas but his ego is really off the charts."

**分析**：Naval的表达风格（压缩金句、不展开）可能被解读为傲慢。

---

**批评3：销售创业主义**
> "His ideas seems useful, but tilted towards selling Entrepreneurship as the only way to become wealthy, and creating the feeling that's his path is a safe bet."

**分析**：批评者认为Naval过度推销创业路径，忽略风险。

### Silicon Valley 历史争议

**Epinions诉讼（2005）**

| 事件 | 细节 |
|------|------|
| **背景** | Epinions与DealTime合并，重组为Shopping.com |
| **争议** | Naval等声称VC隐瞒合并条款、剥夺创始人权益 |
| **结果** | 诉讼和解，Naval成为VC圈"persona non grata" |
| **意义** | 这场诉讼让他看清融资机制黑暗面，为Venture Hacks和AngelList奠定认知基础 |

**媒体报道**：
> "Not so long ago, Naval Ravikant's name was mud in much of Silicon Valley. Radioactive mud." — Silicon Valley Mercury News

> "He went from dot-com pariah to Silicon Valley power broker." — 同上
