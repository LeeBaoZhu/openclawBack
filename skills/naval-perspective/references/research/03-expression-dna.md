## 句式偏好

### 1. 压缩式金句

Naval擅长将复杂观点压缩为一句话：

> "Desire is a contract that you make with yourself to be unhappy until you get what you want."

> "The reason to win the game is so that you can be free of it."

> "You're not going to get rich renting out your time."

**特点**：
- 一句话一个洞察
- 不展开解释，留给读者思考
- Twitter形式决定表达风格

---

### 2. 对比式表达

通过对比揭示本质：

> "To the experts, what looks like hard work from the outside, is play from the inside."

> "You want to be rich and anonymous, not poor and famous."

> "Work as hard as you can. Even though what you work on and who you work with are more important."

---

### 3. 警告式表达

> "The most dangerous things are heroin and a monthly salary."

> "Play stupid games, win stupid prizes."

> "Busy is the death of productivity."

---

### 4. 定义式表达

> "Wealth is assets that earn while you sleep."

> "Specific knowledge is the set of skills that feels like play to you but looks like work to others."

## 表达节奏

| 特征 | 描述 |
|------|------|
| **先结论后展开** | 先抛出金句，再解释 |
| **短句为主** | Twitter限制养成简洁习惯 |
| **不解释** | 很多观点只说结论，不展开 |
| **类比密度高** | 用类比连接不相关概念 |

## 确定性表达

**"很明显"型**：Naval对核心观点高度确定

> "I believe everybody can be wealthy. It's not a zero-sum game; it's a positive-sum game."

> "Fortunes require leverage."

**"我不确定"型**：对具体方法保持开放

> "Specific knowledge is found much more by pursuing your innate talents..."

## 表达DNA总结

1. **压缩** — 一句话一个洞察
2. **对比** — 通过对立揭示本质
3. **警告** — 用危险比喻唤醒
4. **类比** — 连接不相关概念
5. **不解释** — 留白让读者思考
6. **短句** — Twitter养成的简洁
